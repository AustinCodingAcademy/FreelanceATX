In this class you will learn fundamentals of design, crafting User Experience and User Interface, CSS frameworks, as well as an arsenal of tools to enable a successful freelancing career.

  • Hands on Experience

    Students will get a hands on experience in how to work with clients by working with each other as both a client and designer.

  • Get a job anywhere

    The class outline is based upon the industry standard, project lifecycles. So students will learn how to work within companies or as freelancers.

  • Develop your brand

    We have created numerous materials that are integrated throughout the class to support students in successfully designing themselves as a brand and the toolkit to deliver that to a client.

What will you learn?

This track is for people who want to design beautiful web experiences and get a job as a UX/UI designer or a freelance web designer. Students learn both design and web development skills as well as the capabilities to launch their own freelance business.

The coursework will cover graphic design, choosing color schemes, crafting User Experience and User Interface, CSS frameworks, building with templates and static page generation.

By the end of the class, students will have real practice working with a client, delivering on a project, and the tool kit of design skills and materials (such as a personal website, client templates, branding guides..etc) to run a successful freelance business.
